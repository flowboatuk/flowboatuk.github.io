The Kit
What You Get

Included in the Kit

  • 10kW brushless motor with integrated controller
  • Throttle control (pre-wired cable)
  • All necessary wiring and connections
  • Installation documentation
  • Ready to connect to your 48V battery supply

You'll Need Separately

  • 48V battery bank — we can advise on sizing
  • Main battery isolator / disconnect
  • Fuses / circuit protection
  • Wiring from battery to motor

We can supply all additional parts and make up cables to specific lengths — available on request.
